Stablecoin issue and Transfer Smart Contract

2000

This Stablecoin issue and transfer Agreement (the Agreement) sets out the terms and conditions upon which Nordic Energy. Co., Ltd. (Issuer), being a Company duly registered under the laws of Republic of Finland with registered number VAT and having its registered address at Vaasa SmartHub - 1st Floor, Vaasa, Pohjanmaa Finland, will issue and transfer NESC to EPIKTET (Signer).

Therefore, it is hereby agreed as follows:

10%

Yearly Payback
Signer is subjected to transfer a yearly minimum of 10% of the original amount (2000) back to the issuer based on a 100% performance. Transfer can be done either in NESC Stablecoin or in Ether (ETH). Signer is entitled to transfer any higher amount. The contract is fulfilled as soon as 100% ot the original amount got transfered back to the Issuer.

Monthly

Transfer/Payment
Transfer of payments is done on a monthly. Issuer agrees to provide due payment amount not later than 10th day of each month. Signer agrees to schedule payments by end of month.

0.0045 ETH

NESC Nominal Value
For this Stablecoin issue and transfer contract the issuer accepts repayments in NESC Stablecoins or in Ether. In case transfer is done in Ether signer and issuer agreed upon a fixed exchange rate of 0.0045 ETH to 1 NESC. If both parties agree exchange rate of ETH/NESC Stablecoin might be updated with a transaction putting new rate in place after 3 months.

72h/120h

Due Dilligence
Signer aggrees to publish updated ex post performance data not later than 72 hours for the time of the contract. Issuer agrees to validate performance data not later than 120 hours after publishing. In case of late delivery of performance data a warning might be published as a transaction by the Issuer. In case of late delivery of validation a warning might be published as a transaction by the Signer.

80%/20%

Allowance-To-Value Ratio
Signer provides an ongoing allowance 20% of its hold NESC Stablecoin as a locked deposit. Signer is entitled to free transfer/exchange/trade 80% of the open balance (Allowance-To-Value).

30d

Warning period
In case Signer or Issuer does not react on a warning transaction, the transaction sender is entitled to withdraw a penalty fee. Penalty fees are limited to protected value (see Allowance-To-Value ratio). Withdrawal of a penalty does not cover any legal requirements or fines.

6 Months

Public notification period
Signer and Issuer might both agree to cancel obligations made within this issue and transfer contract. Agreement need to be published by both parties as a transaction. Smart Contract ends after a 6 months notice period.
