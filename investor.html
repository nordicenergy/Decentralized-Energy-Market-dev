Nordic Energy

Nordic Energy issues Nordic Energy (NESC) Stablecoin to owners/operators of renewable energy generation facilities (REGF). Extended use of Distributed Ledger Technology ensures autonomous and barrier-free participation to a worldwide energy transition by allowing individuals and corporations at any size to run green energy businesses.

This website provides market monitoring data for NESC. If you have any questions please join our Telegram Channel.
